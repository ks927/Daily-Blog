My Daily Coding Blog

Follow along my journey of coding self-education. I know the best way to get better at something is to do it on a regular basis. So in order to hold myself accountable, I began logging my daily grind in a notebook. After a couple months of pen and paper, I decided to convert ink to html. Most of what you will see here is barebones, bulletpoint summaries of what I spent hours studying. Beginning in January, I've been writing weekly posts about what I'm learning from The Odin Project's curriculum.
